About Me
Hello! My name is Christina and I am a freshmen Computer Science student at the University of Central Florida. My first degree was in nursing, so I currently work as a Registered Nurse at a busy Progressive Care Unit in downtown Orlando. I've always loved nursing and never thought I'd be a programmer, but after graduating from nursing school I had a lot of time to pick up a hobby and my friend suggested I try programming. Before August 2020 I did not even know what programming was and I never considered it as a career option, mostly because I was never exposed to it. After a couple of days watching Youtube tutorials and talking to SWE friends I decided to enroll in an Intro to C course and pursue my next degree in Computer Science. I enjoy Computer Science because it allows me to create fun programs and I love constantly learning. Since I started CS I have been learning C through my coursework and experimenting with Python libraries and HTML/CSS in my free time.
